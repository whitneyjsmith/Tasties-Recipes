Spaghetti Squash Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 1 Spaghetti Squash
  • 1/4 lb sausage
  • 1 c spaghetti sauce
  • 1/2 c shredded cheese
  • Olive oil

Directions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or tinfoil.
2. With a sharp knife, carefully cut spaghetti squash in half lengthwise. With a spoon, gently remove seeds and discard. Generously rub olive oil on each side of the squash. Place spaghetti squash cut side down on baking sheet. Bake for 35 - 45 minutes or until a fork can pierce the shell easily.
3. Cook the sausage while the squash bakes in the oven. After the sausage has browned in the pan, dump it on a plate with a paper towel and pat the grease off.
4. Remove the squash from the oven and allow to cool slightly. With a fork, loosen spaghetti squash strands from shell.
5. Divide the spaghetti sauce and sausage into 2 parts and spoon onto each side of the squash. Sprinkle with shredded cheese.
6. Bake again for 5-7 minutes or until cheese is melted, bubbly, and slightly browned. Serve directly from shell.
