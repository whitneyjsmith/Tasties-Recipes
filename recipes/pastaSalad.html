Pasta Salad

Ingredients

  • 1/2 box of vegetable rotini pasta
  • 1 can sliced olives
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 3 string cheeses
  • Kraft Zesty Italian Dressing

Directions

Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add rotini to water and boil for 8-10 minutes. Meanwhile, chop up cucumber and string cheese into bite size pieces. Drain olives. Once pasta is fully cooked, strain it and dump it into a large bowl. Add olives, chopped cucumber and string cheese. Add enough Kraft Dressing so that everything is coated. Refrigerate and serve.
