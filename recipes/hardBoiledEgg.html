Hard-Boiled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • water
  • ice cubes

Directions

Place eggs in shell in saucepan, add water to cover eggs. Bring to rapid boil over high heat. Reduce heat so that water is just below simmering. Cover the saucepan. Cook 15 to 20 minutes. Pour out the hot water. Fill saucepan with cold water and add ice cubes. Let sit for about 5 minutes. Peel off the shell and use as desired.
