Hamburger Hobo Dinners

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 medium potatoes
  • 4 medium carrots
  • salt and pepper
  • seasoned salt

Directions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Place 1 lb. lean ground beef in a bowl. Shape the ground beef into four hamburger patties. Place the hamburger patties on square foil sheet and season with seasoned salt. Slice onion. Place an onion slice on top of each patty. Peel and slice 4 medium potatoes. Place the sliced potatoes on top of the burger and onion. Peel and slice 4 medium carrots. Add salt and pepper. Fold the foil squares up and seal. Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 30 minutes.
